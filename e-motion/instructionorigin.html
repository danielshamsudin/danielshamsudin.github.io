Instruction

1. In the game there is a paddle and falling objects.

2. When the game start, the game will enable this device's camera and display it on top-right of the game.

3. You need to catch the falling objects by controlling the paddle to earn marks.

4. You need to show your hand on the camera to let it detect your hand and control the paddle

5. The falling object's speed will increase if you reach certain scores.
1000 Scores ->Speed 2
2500 Scores -> Speed 3
4000 Scores -> Speed Max

6, There are three special objects which is:
Boom - Deduct 300 Scores
Green Object - Increase your paddle length for 5 seconds
Red Object - Decrease your paddle length for 5 seconds
