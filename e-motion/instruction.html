Instruction

1. In this game you catch falling objects with a paddle

2. When the game start, the game will enable this device's camera and display it on top-right of the game.

3. To earn marks, catch the objects by controlling the paddle with your hand

4. Show your hand on the camera and move left and right to control the paddle

5. The speed of the object falling will increase once your reach a certain amount of scores, up to 3 times.

6. There are three special objects which is:
Boom - Deduct 300 Scores

Green Object - Increase your paddle length for 5 seconds

Red Object - Decrease your paddle length for 5 seconds
