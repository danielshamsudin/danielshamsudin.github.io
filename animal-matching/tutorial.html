Welcome to our Match-Up game.

1. When there are enough 5 players, clicked on 'JOIN THE GAME' button, you will redirect to this page.

2. Look for other player by interacting with them, check their player id and also the image id that you are looking for!

3. Exchange the image by selecting your own id and also their id.

4. Once confirmed the image you want to exchange, click 'Exchange Now'

5. Complete the image within 10 minutes, you will see this page.

6. To check your ranking, look at the signage board and other player's status.
